Tl;dr

Single indicator occasionally gives trustworthy signals. Reading many indicators at the same time, however, maybe overwhelming. Understanding this, at bitcoinindicated.com, we combine all famous technical analyses into one metric and save you some time! Of course, you can still click on Individual Analyses to see single indicators that we use.

Score Table

We combine Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD), Bollinger Band, Ichimoku Kinko Hyo (Ichimoku Cloud), Relative Strength Index (RSI) indicators into scale of -100 (most likely signal for buying) to 100 (most likely signal for selling).

Score Range Label
-100 to -70 DEFINITELY should BUY
-70 to -40 MAYBE should BUY
-40 to 40 MAYBE should HOLD
40 to 70 MAYBE should SELL
70 to 100 DEFINITELY should SELL
